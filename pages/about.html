Our Story

Crafting Sweet Memories Since 1950

Family Tradition Since 2012

For over seven decades, Pinecrest Bakery has been crafting delicious memories for our community. What started as a small family-owned bakery has blossomed into a beloved institution, while maintaining the same dedication to quality and authentic recipes that made us special from day one.

Every morning, our master bakers arrive before dawn to prepare fresh breads, pastries, and treats using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. We take pride in using only the finest ingredients and creating everything from scratch.
